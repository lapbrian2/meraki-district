# Meraki Road — Image Generation Prompts

## Style DNA: Ink & Ochre Archival

All images are displayed with `filter: grayscale(100%) sepia(20%) contrast(1.1)` and reveal full color on hover. Design images knowing they'll be seen desaturated first.

### Global Style Parameters
- **Mood:** Institutional, contemplative, editorial — never stock, never corporate
- **Lighting:** Directional warm light from upper-left, deep shadows, high contrast
- **Color temp:** Warm neutral — ochres, burnt siennas, warm grays. Avoid pure blue/cool tones
- **Texture:** Subtle film grain, architectural surfaces, raw materials
- **Composition:** Asymmetric, editorial framing, generous negative space
- **No:** People smiling at camera, flat lighting, busy backgrounds, text overlays, stock poses

### Negative prompt (apply to all):
`stock photography, corporate, clip art, cartoon, oversaturated, neon, plastic, glossy, busy background, centered symmetrical composition, text overlay, watermark, low quality, blurry`

## STYLE CONSISTENCY NOTES

1. **All portraits:** 85mm lens equivalent, shallow DoF, warm sidelight, serious/contemplative expression, editorial quality. Kodak Portra 400 film simulation.
2. **All spaces:** No people visible. Warm directional light. Evidence of human activity without human presence. Architectural calm.
3. **All details:** Extreme shallow DoF. Visible texture and material quality. Warm tones. Craftsmanship.
4. **Aspect ratios:** Hero/banner = 16:9 or 21:9. Portraits = 3:4. Cards = 4:5. Square = 1:1.
5. **The sepia filter on the site will warm everything further** — lean slightly cooler than you think. The site does the warming.
