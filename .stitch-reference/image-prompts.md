# Meraki Road — Image Generation Prompts

## Style DNA: Ink & Ochre Archival

All images are displayed with `filter: grayscale(100%) sepia(20%) contrast(1.1)` and reveal full color on hover. Design images knowing they'll be seen desaturated first.

### Global Style Parameters
- **Mood:** Institutional, contemplative, editorial — never stock, never corporate
- **Lighting:** Warm amber, ochre, golden — light as material not physics
- **Color temp:** Warm neutral — ochres, burnt siennas, warm grays, gold. Avoid pure blue/cool tones
- **Texture:** Organic-to-architectural morphing, membrane surfaces, warm luminous material
- **Composition:** Asymmetric, generous negative space, warm void as active element
- **No:** People smiling at camera, flat lighting, busy backgrounds, text overlays, stock poses, photorealistic simulation

### Negative prompt (apply to all):
`stock photography, corporate, clip art, cartoon, oversaturated, neon, plastic, glossy, busy background, centered symmetrical composition, text overlay, watermark, low quality, blurry, photorealistic, conventional portrait`

## STYLE CONSISTENCY NOTES

1. **All portraits:** AI art — figures merging with their medium, warm abstract environments, not photographic simulation.
2. **All spaces:** Abstract warm environments. No literal rooms. Warm void, floating objects, luminous membranes.
3. **All details:** Macro surfaces as warm landscapes. Ink as terrain. Paper as geology. Material as territory.
4. **Aspect ratios:** Hero/banner = 16:9 or 21:9. Portraits = 3:4. Cards = 4:5. Square = 1:1.
5. **The sepia filter on the site will warm everything further** — lean slightly cooler than you think. The site does the warming.
6. **Core palette:** Amber, ochre, burnt sienna, gold, warm charcoal. No cool tones. Every image should feel like it was rendered in firelight.
